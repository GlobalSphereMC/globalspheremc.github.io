Applications

If you wish to apply for the moderator job, please fill out the application. If you are accepted, you will be granted the rank of "helper" for a month or so as a trial. You will then either be promoted to moderator or rejected.

If you want to be considered as a news reporter, please DM the owner (armeniyan#1968) on discord.
