What's up at GlobalSphere?

December 2nd, 2022.

GlobalSphere is now launching the brewery feature!

The brewery plugin allows you to brew all kinds of custom drinks and ferment many ingredients.

Our almanac features a list of all of our custom ingredients and custom drinks. HOWEVER, some parts of the recipe book were old and unintelligible! Due to this, we unfortunately can only provide some information to you. Maybe one day you can experiment and repair the recipe book?
