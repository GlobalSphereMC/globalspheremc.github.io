What's up at GlobalSphere?

December 7th, 2022.

We have updated the GlobalSphere datapack with new features!

The main additions are the new custom recipes for the sponge, cobweb, and deepslate blocks! These work on both Bedrock and Java editions.

The smaller (but still great) feature is a custom loot table for the drowned mob! In addition to their regular drops, they now have a chance of dropping prismarine shards and/or prismarine crystals.

For more information on these features and to see our custom crafting recipes, please see the almanac.
