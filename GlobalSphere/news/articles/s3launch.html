What's up at GlobalSphere?

March 1st, 2024.

After all the troubles that we went through with FalixNodes, we have finally found a new host and decided to start up GlobalSphere Season 3. Today is the launch date!

Changes from S2

  • Coal, iron, gold, lapis, and diamond ores now drop "chunks" or "carats" - 9 of these combined creates one product. Fortune buffs the amount of chunks you get.
  • A money limit now exists - once you exceed $5000 generated in a single day, your income is multiplied by 0.1.
  • Nether portals may not be created anymore - there is a single one hidden somewhere in the world. The beginner's guide will be updated shortly with hints on how to get there.
  • Outposts may now be made anywhere.
