What's up at GlobalSphere?

April 22nd, 2023.

Today is the day that the long-awaited steel update is releasing! This update is the first major one to use our new modding capabilities that our developer has created. This update makes many major changes to the gameplay, and we are closely monitoring how players interact with the game after this update to make changes if necessary.

Brewery Balance Changes

Before we jump into the meat of the update, let's quickly go over a change to one of the brewery drinks:

  • Crimson Tea: Strength 4 (10s) -> Haste 2 (30s)

We made this change due to crimson tea being ridiculously overpowered. However, as people probably brewed a lot of crimson tea, we are keeping its combat usefulness by replacing the damage boost with an attack speed boost.

Steel

Unlike conventional steel, this steel is a mixture of iron, carbon, and... ancient debris. Steel is way better than iron, and can be used to build more modern machinery.

  • 4 ancient debris + 2 iron ingots + 2 coal -> 6 steel ingots

Armor Upgrades

Regular iron armor has become more and more irrelevant with all of the new ways to boost your damage. Due to this, we will allow you to upgrade your iron armor into steel armor! Wearing the set gives you the resistance I effect permanently (until you take it off). To upgrade your armor, hold the desired piece of armor in your hand and do /upgradearmor. You need at least 4 steel ingots in your inventory to perform this upgrade. All enchantments on your armor will be kept when upgrading.

Radios

The radio is a fun little item. When right clicked, it tells you one of 6 random messages. These messages change every now and then. Sometimes, future update hints may be broadcasted to radios!

  • 5 steel ingots + 2 gold ingots + 1 redstone -> 1 radio

Ranged Weaponry

With steel, you can now craft guns. There are currently 2 types of guns, but later we may also add other things, like turrets or grappling hooks.

Note: all damage listed is in half-hearts, with no armor, resistance, or other effects taken into account.

Ammo

To make the game simpler, there is only one type of ammo. Each gunshot consumes one ammo.

  • 2 steel ingots + 1 gold ingot -> 20 ammo

Pistol

The pistol does 19 damage and can fire every 0.5 seconds.

  • 2 steel ingots + 1 redstone -> 1 pistol

Sniper Rifle

The sniper rifle is a long ranged gun that fires up to every 4 seconds. Up close, it deals 12 damage, but far away, it deals 30 damage.

  • 3 steel ingots + 1 redstone + 1 glass pane -> 1 sniper rifle
